Tong Duy Son

I am currently a PhD student, Marie Curie fellow at MECO group, Department of Mechanical Engineering, KULeuven, supervised by Prof. Jan Swevers and Prof. Goele Pipeleers.

After 6 years working in the field of automatic control, I have gained a broad knowledge in many aspects of control theory (linear and nonlinear control, robust control, optimal control...), control design techniques (learning control, PID, loop-shaping, mu-synthesis, MIMO control, feedforward...), and numerical optimization (i.e. convex optimization, LMI).

As a control engineer, I am skilled with programming languages (i.e. MATLAB, LabVIEW, LaTeX), and experience with real-time implementation (i.e. dSPACE, Simulink) of control implementation and system identification.

Interests: Control theory and applications, mechatronic systems, precision control, autonomous vehicles, control for manufacturing
